Here are some random facts I've discovered!

  1. Coloured diamonds are more rare & expensive than white diamonds. To be specific red & pink diamonds are the most rare and sought after in the coloured diamond category.
  2. Pearls are the only gemstones that are sourced from a living creature.
  3. In the jewellery trade the most popular gold band for diamond engagement rings is White Gold, as it enhances the colour and clarity of the diamond, unlike a Yellow Gold which will take give the diamond a slight yellowish undertone!
